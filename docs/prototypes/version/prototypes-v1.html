Operational dashboard for submission review, lifecycle management, and permission-safe curation.

Data state
Design fit: Suitable when review quality, submission safety, admin throughput, and member ownership are the main risk areas for an internal, login-gated company tool. The same console reshapes itself for members (a department-scoped catalog plus their own submissions) and admins (full curation and department assignment), while the production app must enforce the same permission and visibility boundaries server-side. Not ideal as a marketing-first public homepage, because it is access-controlled and prioritizes operations over browsing delight and SEO.
